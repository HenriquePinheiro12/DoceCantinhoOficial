    Who We Are

    Doce Cantinho is a familial store created in 2019.
    We are a pair of mother and daughter which loves confectionery and has a lot of passion for what it does.

    Our History

    The store was created from its idealizer´s chilhood´s dream, who´ve always loved to confection candies for family and friends.

    In two years of history, it turned into profession what was once just a hobby, applying all the learning and study that have been developed throughout the last times.

    Our deliciousness

    From tasty cakes to irresistible ice creams, not forgetting of our delicious donuts!
